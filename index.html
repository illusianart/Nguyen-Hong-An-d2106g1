APPROX 7600

PRELUDE

We’re never sure of what the future holds. Maybe a couple of years from now, the world will be nothing but ruined cities and abandoned areas. The reckless life has finally taken a toll on every single one of us.

PRELUDE – the story is told from the individual perspective. Inspired by the last 2-year quarantine, PRELUDE was demonstrated as the post-apocalypse world – a world with almost no sign of old civilization.

In celebration of Riot’s 10th anniversary, we hope this campaign can somehow, give you a headsup of our next transition into something new.
